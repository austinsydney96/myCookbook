Pork Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 Poblano Pepper
  • 1 Roma Tomato
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • Taco Seasoning
  • 1 Jalapeno
  • 1 Lime
  • 1/2 lb of Ground Pork
  • Sour Cream
  • 6 tortillas

Directions

  1. Wash and dry all produce then finely dice the tomato, garlic, yellow onion and jalapeno
  2. cut the lime into quarter pieces
  3. in a small bowl combine 2tbs of the onion, jalapeno, a squeeze of lime juice and salt and pepper to taste
  4. In a second small bowl combine sour cream, a pinch of garlic and a squeeze of lime juice with salt and pepper
  5. Heat a large drizzle of oil in a pan over medium-heat adding pork, cooking for 5-6 minutes. The stir in remaining onion, jalapeno, taco seasoning for 3-4 minutes until the pork is browned.
  6. Whie pork cooks heat the tortillas in damp papertowls in the microwave until they're warm and pliable.
  7. Divide the tortillas and fill with the park mixture, pico de gallo and sour cream.
